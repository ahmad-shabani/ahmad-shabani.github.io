01 · Bio

Biography

I am an Assistant Professor at Amirkabir University of Technology and has recently joined KTH University as a visiting researcher funded by WASP. My ongoing research primarily focuses on existing vulnerabilities against micro-architectural attacks particularly devoted to finding GPU and CPU side and covert channel attacks. I also have several years of industrial experiences in digital ASIC design and HW-SW implementation for FPGAs and embedded systems.

My research interests include broader aspects in hardware security and trust domain such as hardware Trojans, PUF-based authentication, and side-channel aware implementation of digital systems.
