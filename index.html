Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher at KTH Royal Institute of Technology working in hardware and computer security. My research connects GPU and processor security, microarchitectural side channels, Hardware Trojan detection, PUF-based authentication, and secure digital system design.

Before joining KTH, I served as an Assistant Professor at Amirkabir University of Technology and worked on digital ASIC design, embedded systems, FPGA-based platforms, and security-aware engineering projects.
