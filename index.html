Academic timeline

Ahmad Shabani is a researcher in hardware and computer security and digital ASIC design. His work focuses on efficient countermeasures against Hardware Trojans, lightweight PUF-based authentication protocols, and security analysis across multiple abstraction levels.

His academic path connects rigorous doctoral research in digital electronic systems with faculty teaching, industrial ASIC projects, embedded systems, and current postdoctoral research at KTH Royal Institute of Technology.
